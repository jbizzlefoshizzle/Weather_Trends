Looking at Cloudiness by Latitude

Visualizations


Using Jupyter Notebook, I was able to randomly generate latitude and longitude coordinates for 566 unique cities.

At the time of querying the OpenWeatherMap API, 73 countries reported 100% cloudiness.

23 countries reported 0% cloudiness.

Cloudiness does not seem to be correlated with latitude.
